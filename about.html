How to play

Welcome to Simple Blackjack! The goal is to get your hand total as close to 21 as possible, without going over. Here's how it works:

Card Values:

  • Number Cards (2-9, and the '10'): Count as their face value.
  • Face Cards (Jack, Queen, King): Each counts as 10.
  • Ace: Counts as 11 initially. If your total sum exceeds 21, an Ace's value will automatically change to 1 if that prevents you from busting.

Gameplay:
1. Click "START GAME" on the main page. You'll be dealt two cards.
2. If your sum is less than 21, the message "Do you want to draw a new card?" appears. You can click "NEW CARD" to get another card (this is called a "Hit").
3. Winning & Losing:
  • If your sum is exactly 21 (Blackjack!), you win that round.
  • If your sum goes over 21 (Bust!), you're out for that round.
  • You can keep drawing cards as long as your sum is below 21.

Good luck!
